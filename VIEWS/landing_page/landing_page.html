Why We Built AquaRoute

Solving the lack of visibility in water transportation

Lack of Visibility

Passengers wait blindly without knowing the ferry's location.

Fragmented Info

Updates scattered across social media are hard to find.

Weather Uncertainty

Poor decision-making during storms due to lack of data.

No Visual ETA

Unlike land apps, ferry passengers can't track arrival times.

Unpredicted Delays

Crowding at ports when delays aren't communicated early.

Manual Advisories

Text-based updates are hard to understand at a glance.
